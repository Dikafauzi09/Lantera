Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a complaint? ‚ñº

Most complaints are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Complex issues may require 3-5 business days for thorough investigation. You will receive email updates throughout the process.

Can I track my complaint status? ‚ñº

Yes! Use the "Track Status" tab and enter your reference number to check the current status of your complaint at any time.

Why do I need to select a location on the map? ‚ñº

The location helps us route your complaint to the appropriate department and enables faster response times. You can click anywhere on the map or use your current location.

What types of files can I upload? ‚ñº

You can upload images (JPG, PNG), PDF documents, and Word documents (DOC, DOCX). Each file should be under 5MB in size.

Will I receive a confirmation email? ‚ñº

Yes, you will receive an automated confirmation email immediately after submitting your complaint, along with your unique reference number.

Can I update my complaint after submission? ‚ñº

If you need to add additional information, please contact us with your reference number via email or phone, and we'll update your complaint record.

What happens after I submit a complaint? ‚ñº

Your complaint is immediately logged in our system and assigned to the appropriate department. Our team will investigate the issue and contact you with a resolution or request for additional information if needed.
